FAQs

How do I make a purchase?

To place an order, follow the steps & place your order through our Website

What payment methods do MediCare accept?

We got all sorts of Payments to offer you ! COD available Island-wide. Bank Payments / Online Bank Transfers / Payment Link or simply order through our Website & use your Shopping Card.

Can I ship to multiple addresses?

Apologies, but one Order will only go to the given specific Address, if in case you need to send a Gift to any loved ones, we will send to any address you provide without the Invoice Printed on.

How long does a delivery take, and what are the charges?

Our Standard delivery is within 3-5 working days, but packages sent to Col 01-15 will only take 1-3 working days. Standard Fee will be 250/= for any destination & 350/= for Outstation ( Northern Province Areas )
